Frequently Asked Questions

Are these helmets certified for safety?

No. HelmetMax helmets are strictly for decorative use only. They are not DOT/Snell approved and offer no protection for motorcycle riding or racing. They are designed 100% for aesthetic display.

What materials are used?

We use high-grade ABS composites for the shell to ensure durability and a perfect finish, with polycarbonate for the visors and soft foam padding for the interior lining to maintain the realistic look.

How long does shipping take?

Standard models ship within 24-48 hours. Custom orders require 5-7 business days for production and quality control before dispatch.
